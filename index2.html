About this Idea

Ultras Rossoneri Ezra Prasetya Faculty of Business and Management Economics Student ID (NPM): 250328846 I created this 4-in-1 website to integrate Bookshelf, SIAKAD, SIATMA, SIKMA, and Web Kuliah into a single platform. The purpose is to simplify access, improve efficiency, and save time for students by gathering all essential academic systems in one page. This idea comes from my personal initiative as a student who values practicality and productivity in managing academic activities.
